Content

Home page

Do you know the benefits of having plants in your home and organic crops?
That is the objective of my website, an easy-to-navigate and very intuitive website where you can buy everything you need to create natural spaces full of harmony and improve the quality of life in your home. On my website you will find a catalog of plants, you can appreciate the beauty of the plants in the gallery and find a guide about how to take care of them, discover how to improve your home through contact with nature on my website. The importance of plants to humans cannot be underestimated. Without them, neither we nor most animal species could exist, it is of great importance in medicine, in the wood and paper industry, in the food industry, plants are necessary for our lives, on this page we can learn more about plants and how to benefit more from them. a study published in 2019 by the journal Nature Ecology Evolution, showed that:

-total of 571 plant species have become extinct.
-Every year at least 2 species become extinct.
-Hawaii is the place with the most extinct plants, followed by South Africa, Mauritius, Australia, India, Brazil and Madagascar.
-Some of the extinct plants are part of cultivated species, so they were never introduced into nature.

-Most extinct species are woody plants and come from the tropics or subtropics.

With this information we can conclude that the extinction of plants generates significant damage to the environment and the rest of the living beings that make life on planet Earth. The care of the species is our responsibility. On the home page, some images of landscapes full of plants and life will be shown, there will be a summary of the importance of plants on the planet and in the history of humanity, types of plants, people will prefer to visit this page since in it you can find information in an agile and fast way about each plant that exists in the catalog, it will be a plant wiki with useful information regarding each plant.
